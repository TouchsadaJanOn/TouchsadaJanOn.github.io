The vision of the Greeley Fire Department is to serve Greeley and its people. Each person is dedicated to serving with honor, integrity, and respect. During disastrous events under pressure and stress, where human life is the number one priority, safety is still the primary procedure in order to efficiently maintain the situation and keep the situation under control.

The firefighter will help and guide you through the following listed below in order to make sure your and your spouse's safety

  • Fire code compliance
  • New construction permits and plan review
  • Fire protection systems permits and plan review
  • Development review
  • Fire flow information
  • Fire safety education
