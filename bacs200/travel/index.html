FEATURETTES

Diving Beginner! It’ll blow your mind!

Beginner class will start at a basic level where you won't have to go too deep in the water. You will learn how to safely operate the oxygen tank and adapt being under water. Don't worry, you will still get to see plenty of beautiful sea life!

Intermediate Class See for yourself.

Intermediate class will be diving deeper into the ocean and see creature who live deeper than the surface. This require for you to know the basics. Deep down, you will experience sea animal that live below 60 feet and up!

Advanced Class Checkmate.

Here, you will sole be diving with the instructor, you will go 100 feet below the surface! Recommand taking beginner and intermediate before taking this class
